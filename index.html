GOOD MANNERS

Good manners are polite and kind actions that can help you and others feel better. They can include:


Being respectful:
Saying "please" and "thank you", waiting your turn, and apologizing when you make a mistake

Being considerate:
Making eye contact, listening carefully, and being helpful to others

Being responsible:
Being a team player, responding to communication in a timely manner, and avoiding profanity

Being friendly:
Greeting people, smiling, and speaking highly of others

Being clean:
Maintaining cleanliness at home, school, and other public places
